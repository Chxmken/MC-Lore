Chapter Eight

Company

Being full now, the chicken waddled towards the boy and sat next to it, leaning on the boy as if cuddling and proceeded to fall asleep. The boy was surprised to see it fall asleep next to him. He decided it was a good idea to not have eaten the chicken, considering the events that had taken place, he was comforted by the company from the chicken, he wondered if the chicken would have left by the morning as he laid down to fall asleep on the dirt, looking up at the sky full of stars.
