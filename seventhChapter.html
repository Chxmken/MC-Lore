Chapter Seven

Mushroom-continued

It looked at the bowl for a few minutes, curious for the way it smelled like mushrooms even though no mushroom was seen on the bowl, and cautious in case this was a trap from the boy. It looked up from the bowl to the boy, contemplating whether or not to give it a try, seeing if there were any malicious intentions coming from the boy. The boy looked back at the chicken, wondering if it was going to eat or not, no longer looking at it with the intention to eat it, he was pleased enough with the mushroom stew. The chicken, sensing the boy had no malicious intent, hovered over the bowl before slowly drinking it. It tasted delicious, so it stopped being cautious and started devouring the stew. The boy looked bewildered at the chicken devouring the stew. Barely a few minutes had passed when the chicken looked up from the bowl, with stars in its eyes and seeming to be in a happy mood.
